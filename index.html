Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my subscription?
Open the app → Profile → Subscriptions. For billing issues contact support@marinasfitnest.example.
App crashes on startup — what do I do?
Try reinstalling the app, restart the device, and if it persists send a bug report with logs and device model.
How can I request a new feature?
Send a short description of the feature and use cases to support. We collect community requests for prioritization.
